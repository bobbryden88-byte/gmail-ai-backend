Welcome to Gmail AI Assistant

Get started in 3 simple steps

1

Click the extension icon

Find the Gmail AI Assistant icon in your browser toolbar and click it to open the extension popup.

2

Create your AI assistant account

Create an account or sign in to start using AI-powered email assistance.

3

Start composing an email and click "AI Assist"

Open Gmail compose, write your email, and click the "AI Assist" button to get AI-generated responses.
